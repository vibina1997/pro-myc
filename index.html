Promyc® as an Industrial Ingredient

Promyc® is a vegan mycoprotein ingredient produced through biotechnology with the fermentation process as a core. Our production process allows us to be a lot more efficient than traditional farming in terms of time, cost and feedstock, with much less environmental impact. It also enables the decentralization of food production, making it possible to produce Promyc® wherever needed.

PROMYC

Promyc® is in fact quite a boring ingredient – neutral colour, taste, and smell. But boring doesn’t necessarily mean a bad thing. Besides being neutral in colour, taste, and smell, Promyc is a sustainable mycoprotein ingredient that can be used to produce vegan food products and that brings a great source of fibre and protein, nutrition, and healthy vitamins while offering a great texture.

Promyc® as a Semi-finished Product

Besides a sustainable ingredient, Promyc® also comes in a wide range of delicious ready-to-cook products. Nuggets, Crispy Burger, Vego Balls, and Schnitzel – all made from Promyc® with the ability to offer ready-to-cook products to finally – be ready to eat.

Crispy Burger

A true favourite! With its firm texture and its juiciness, the crispy burger is your ultimate vegan and gluten-free option for breaded chicken. Since optimally prepared directly from the freezer, your sustainable comfort food is just minutes away!

Tôna Flakes

Our Tôna flakes have a fishy and fibrous texture, perfect for salads, creamy toppings or simply serve as it is. We strongly recommend trying it out as a layer in your Smörgåstårta - we have tried it, and it’s delicious!

Nuggets

Delicious golden nuggets, coated in crispy gluten free breading and with a chewy bite like that of chicken. Convinced you would pass a blind test? Find out by trying them!

Vego Balls

Savoury Promyc® balls, seasoned with allspice and nutmeg to offer you the vegan version of the Swedish “Husmanskost”. Best served with potatoes, cream sauce, and lingonberries.

Schnitzel

With a bite and the taste of a classic veal schnitzel, our sustainable Promyc-based option is covered in gluten-free breading and is suitable for vegans. Whether you want to serve it with classic toppings, like capers and lemon, or slice it up Japanese katsu style is entirely up to you.
